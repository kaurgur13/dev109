Week Starting 1/13/2025
Day of the Week Recipe Name Instructions
Monday Vegetarian Tacos Recipe #001: Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook black beans with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper for 5-7 minutes. Warm tortillas in a skillet or microwave. Assemble tacos by adding beans to tortillas, then top with lettuce, tomatoes, onion, avocado, and cilantro. Add sour cream (optional) and serve with lime wedges.
Tuesday Greek Salad Recipe #002: Chop cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and bell pepper into bite-sized pieces. Combine vegetables in a bowl with olives and feta cheese. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss and serve chilled.
Wednesday Grilled Chicken and Rice Bowl Recipe #003: Grill chicken breast seasoned with salt, pepper, and olive oil until fully cooked. Cook rice according to package instructions. Slice the grilled chicken and place it over the cooked rice. Add your choice of veggies (e.g., broccoli, bell peppers, or corn). Drizzle with your favorite sauce or dressing and serve.
Thursday Grilled Cheese with Tomato Soup Recipe #004: Butter two slices of bread and place cheese between them. Grill the sandwich in a pan over medium heat until golden and the cheese melts. Heat canned tomato soup on the stove or in the microwave. Serve the grilled cheese with a bowl of hot tomato soup.
Friday Cauliflower Rice Stir-fry Recipe #005: Grate or pulse cauliflower florets in a food processor to make "rice." Heat oil in a pan and sauté diced onions, garlic, and any veggies of your choice. Add cauliflower rice to the pan and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Garnish with green onions and serve.
Saturday Penne Arrabbiata Recipe #006: Cook penne pasta according to package instructions. Heat olive oil in a pan, add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for 1-2 minutes. Add canned crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce, garnish with fresh basil or parsley. Serve with grated Parmesan (optional).
Sunday Turkey and Avocado Sandwich Recipe #007: Toast two slices of whole-grain bread. Layer slices of turkey, avocado, and lettuce on one slice of bread. Add tomato slices and a bit of mustard or mayo (optional). Top with the second slice of bread and serve.
